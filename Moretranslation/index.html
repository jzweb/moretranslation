About



MORE TRANSLATIONS as an excellent alternative to handle the translation of your medical documents of clinical and hospital use such as DIAGNOSTICS, PRE AND POST SURGERY INSTRUCTIONS, SPECIAL CARE INSTRUCTIONS, DIVERSE THERAPIES, RESEARCH STUDIES, RECOMMENDATIONS, REGISTRATION FORMS, APPLICATIONS FOR DIFFERENT PURPOSES, HANDOUTS and all kinds of printed material that are used on a daily basis in every health care facility.

Our company specializes on translating Medical as well as Educational Documents and is already providing its services to a variety of offices in our state, such as the UNIVERSITY OF UTAH HEALTH CARE, SALT LAKE SCHOOL DISTRICT, CANYONS SCHOOL DISTRICT, we have rendered translations for the UTAH HEALTH DEPARTMENT, and our renderings are accepted by the INS, DMV and other official entities, we are registered in the State of Utah Division of Commerce.

Our translators are members of the AMERICAN TRANSLATORS ASSOCIACION (ATA); we use the strictest guidelines of professional translating and subject our work to the deepest grammatical and orthographical analysis, ensuring a literal transmission of the message and the easy and full understanding of it, by target readers from all ethnic and cultural Spanish speaking backgrounds.

MORETRANSLATIONS, uses modern technology, sends the translation requests to our professional translators using servers, they process them immediately, we characterize for the speed in delivering a high Quality work. We are ready to take on any size of document on any of the Windows or Mac formats, we will deliver your documents on any of this preset formats and will leave the original formatting, with the exception of the graphics, unless requested otherwise. We will deliver your translations via email, but could also print copies or save your work on other kinds of media per request.
